Why Me?

Most businesses don't need a $5,000 agency website. They need something clean, fast, and actually finished — a site that looks professional, works on mobile, and doesn't disappear into a revision cycle that takes three months. That's what I build.

I'm a student developer, which means my prices are low and I'm genuinely motivated to do good work — I'm building a reputation, not just cashing a check. Every site I make is hand-coded from scratch: real HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, not a Wix template with a custom font slapped on it. You can see the difference.

I keep things straightforward. You tell me what your business does, what you need on the site, and I build it. No confusing proposals, no hidden costs, no disappearing after the site goes live. If something needs fixing, I fix it.

If you're a local business that's been putting off getting a website — or you have one that looks like it was built in 2009 — I'm a pretty good place to start.
